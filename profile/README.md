## Overview

Reeder Advanced is a beautiful RSS news reading application that provides a clean, distraction-free reading environment for following websites, blogs, newsletters, and any content source that publishes an RSS or Atom feed.

The reading view strips away the visual noise of the original website and presents article content in a clean, typographically optimized format that is comfortable for extended reading sessions without the distraction of ads, navigation, and other page elements.

Feed management with folders, tags, and smart filters organizes subscriptions into a structured library that makes navigating a large collection of followed sources fast and the discovery of unread content immediate.

Offline reading support downloads article content for reading without an active internet connection, making your reading library accessible in any environment regardless of connectivity status.

For readers who want to follow a curated set of websites and publications on their own terms rather than through algorithmically curated social media feeds, Reeder Advanced is the RSS application that provides a clean, controlled, and genuinely enjoyable reading experience.

## Performance

Reeder balances performance with system resource usage intelligently. Operations are handled in the background, keeping your Mac responsive for everything else. Efficient memory management and smart caching reduce overhead during extended work sessions.

Apple Silicon acceleration is fully utilized on M-series Macs, delivering noticeably faster performance. On Intel machines, hardware acceleration is used wherever available.
